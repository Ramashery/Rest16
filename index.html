Truffle Ravioli

€28
Primi

Our signature truffle ravioli is a celebration of Italian culinary artistry. Handcrafted daily using traditional techniques, each delicate pasta parcel is filled with a luxurious blend of ricotta, parmigiano reggiano, and black truffle from the Piedmont region. Served in a velvety sage-infused butter sauce and finished with shavings of fresh Alba white truffle, this dish offers an earthy complexity balanced by the creaminess of the filling.

Ingredients

Fresh Egg Pasta
Black Truffle
Ricotta Cheese
Parmigiano Reggiano
White Alba Truffle
Sage Butter Sauce
Organic Free-range Eggs
Semolina Flour

Pairing Recommendations

Barolo, 2015 Chianti Classico Riserva Pinot Nero

Chef's Notes

"This dish was inspired by my grandmother's kitchen in Tuscany, where truffles were a seasonal treasure. We've elevated her rustic recipe with modern techniques while preserving the soul of the original. The key is using the freshest truffles and allowing their aroma to shine through without overpowering the delicate pasta. Each bite should transport you to an autumn forest in Northern Italy."
